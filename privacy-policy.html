CardSnaply

Last updated: 18 November 2025

CardSnaply is a business card scanning app. This app uses your device camera to scan business cards and extract contact details for you.

1. Information We Collect

  • Camera images: When you scan a card, the app uses the camera to capture an image of the card.
  • Contact details: The app reads text from the card (such as name, phone, email) and shows it to you so you can save it to your contacts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • We only use the camera image to read the text on the card and fill the fields in the app.
  • The contact details are only used to create or update contacts on your device.

3. Data Storage and Sharing

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your contact details with third parties.
  • The processing is only for helping you manage your own contacts.

4. Permissions

  • Camera: Used only to scan business cards.
  • Storage/Photos (if requested): Used only if you choose an existing photo of a card from your gallery.

5. Children's Privacy

CardSnaply is not designed for children under 13.
